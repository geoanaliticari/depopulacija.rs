Road Network Density

The indicator Road Network Density shows the status of the road network in the area. To calculate the indicator, different datasets were used: OpenStreetMap (status of 2020) and administrative division (GeoSrbija [Open data of the National Data Infrastructure, Republic Geodetic Authority]). To illustrate the relationship with population dynamics, this indicator is overlapped with the Population Change Index 2015/1975 (dataset GHS-POP R2019A - GHS population grid multitemporal [European Commission, Joint Research Center), 2019]) in order to establish in which areas road infrastructure played an important role in population changes. In a broader context, in the earlier period of intensive internal migrations stimulated by urbanization and economic progress, in some areas road accessibility was one of the associated factors that catalysed depopulation.
